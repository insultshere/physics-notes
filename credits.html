These notes are highly based off of Halliday, Resnick, and Krane’s book Physics, and Ling, Sanny, and Moeb's book University Physics, under OpenStax.
You should read those for more information.

Questions (which are concept-based), Exercises (which require direct application of a concept), and Problems (which require a non-direct approach) are taken from the two aforementioned books. They are credited with the following format:
(Publisher) (Type of Question)(Chapter).(Number)
For example,
  • LSM CQ3.5 refers to Ling, Sanny, and Moeb's 5th Conceptual Question under the 3rd chapter
  • HRK E2.11 refers to Halliday, Resnick, and Krane's 11th Exercise under the 2nd chapter
  • LSM P3.90a refers to Part A of Ling, Sanny, and Moeb's 90th Problem under the 3rd chapter
Some examples are made by me, and are symbolized with the (✎) symbol.
