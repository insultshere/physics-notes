This branch has a prerequisite: Dynamics.

Energy is a conserved quantity that is incredibly important to multiple branches of Physics. In this topic we discuss work, kinetic and potential energy, and the conservation of mechanical energy. We then use these quantities to solve problems where Newton's laws are too hard to apply in.
