This branch has a prerequisite: Dynamics.

Despite the power of Newton's laws, we still lack the abilities to analyze situations which involve collisions. In this topic, we develop the concepts of momentum, impulse, and the conservation of momentum. We can then use these concepts to solve problems involving types of collisions.
